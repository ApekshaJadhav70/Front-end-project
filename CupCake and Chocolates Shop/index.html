Ingredients: Coconut, egg whites, sugar, cocoa, vanilla, salt, melted chocolate. Instructions: Mix all ingredients, scoop onto a baking sheet, bake at 325°F (163°C) for 18-20 mins, cool, drizzle with chocolate, and enjoy!

Vanilla Choco Chips Cookies

Mix 1 cup flour, ½ tsp baking soda, ¼ tsp salt.Cream ½ cup butter, ½ cup sugar, ¼ cup brown sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, 1 egg.Combine all, fold in ½ cup choco chips. Scoop, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 mins. Enjoy!

Cocoa Choco Chips Cookies

Mix 1 cup flour, ¼ cup cocoa powder, ½ tsp baking soda, ¼ tsp salt.Cream ½ cup butter, ½ cup sugar, ¼ cup brown sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, 1 egg. Combine all, fold in ½ cup choco chips.Scoop, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 mins. Enjoy
